# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge
We as members, contributors, and leaders pledge to make participation in the BlackhawkBraids community a harassment-free experience for everyone.

## Our Standards
Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism

## Enforcement Responsibilities
Organization maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing our standards of acceptable behavior.

## Scope
This Code of Conduct applies within all community spaces and also applies when an individual is officially representing the organization.

## Enforcement
Instances of abusive, harassing, or otherwise unacceptable behavior may be reported by contacting support@blackhawkbraids.com.
