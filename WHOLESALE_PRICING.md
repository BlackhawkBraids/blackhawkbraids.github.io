# Wholesale Pricing — Bulk Paracord Bracelet Orders

Thank you for your interest in wholesale purchasing from BlackhawkBraids! We offer tiered pricing to reward bulk orders and make it easy for retailers, event organizers, and group buyers to stock up.

## Pricing Tiers

| Tier | Quantity | Price per Bracelet | Savings vs. Retail |
|------|----------|-------------------|---------------------|
| Retail | 1–9 | $12.00 | — |
| Bronze | 10–24 | $9.60 | 20% off |
| Silver | 25–49 | $8.40 | 30% off |
| Gold | 50–99 | $7.20 | 40% off |
| Platinum | 100+ | $6.00 | 50% off |

*All prices are in USD per bracelet. Retail price is based on our standard single-unit price.*

## Order Requirements

- **Minimum wholesale order:** 10 bracelets
- **Mixed styles allowed:** Quantity discounts apply to the total order, not per style
- **Custom colors/patterns:** Available for Gold and Platinum tier orders (50+ units); please inquire for lead times

## How to Place a Wholesale Order

1. **Email us** at wholesale@blackhawkbraids.com with your desired quantity, styles, and color preferences.
2. We will send a **quote and invoice** within 1–2 business days.
3. Payment is due upon invoice receipt before production begins.
4. Standard **production time** is 5–10 business days after payment, depending on order size.
5. Orders ship via USPS Priority Mail or UPS Ground; bulk shipping rates apply.

## Terms & Conditions

- Prices are subject to change; the price in effect at the time of invoice applies.
- No returns or exchanges on wholesale orders unless items are defective.
- Resellers must comply with BlackhawkBraids brand guidelines.

For questions, contact us at wholesale@blackhawkbraids.com or visit our [organization profile](https://github.com/BlackhawkBraids).
